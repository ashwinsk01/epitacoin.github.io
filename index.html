A bit about OUR Project

Epicoin is a block-chain system that is designed to help solve NP–Complete problems. As one may expect, the name ”Epicoin” is the contraction of the names ”EPITA” and ”Bitcoin”. Epicoin can be seen as a network of nodes, with each node competing to solve the current NP-Complete problem first. The winning node is rewarded one unit of the Epicoin currency, as well as the right to choose what will the whole network work on next.One way to look at Epicoin is to see it as an alternative to cluster computing.
Epicoin’s goal is to make its users invest their computational resources in the solving of a real-life problems, but unlike classic cluster-computing, the nodes would be in competition instead of in cooperation.
